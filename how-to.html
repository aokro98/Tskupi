What is Tskupi?


Tskupi is an application that’s meant to encourage human connection and creativity anywhere, any time, by enabling and making easier participating in games and playful activities. It’s essentially meant to assist with real-life party games, using functions like Dice Roll and Coin Flip, but can also provide entire games, such as Alias.

How do I use Tskupi?


You can use Tskupi as a party game assistant, or as a game provider in itself. For example, Tskupi can roll dice for you when you're playing a board game, or provide games that you can play only with your device and a couple friends.

Rules

Here are some guidelines you can use to make playing more fun! (P.S. For now, only Alias guidelines are provided. Stay tuned for Truth or Dare and Imposter!)

★ Alias

When playing Alias, you can use any strategy you’d like; you can play in pairs, and have the winner be whoever finishes guessing words first, or play as a group, and move on from a word once any single person has guessed the word the player chosen for the round is trying to convey.

You can try to convey words using gestures or verbally. I advise that you try not have your friends guess the words with phonetically similar sounds, or by saying the word in a different language!

As a best practice, don’t focus too much on whether the word a player has tried to guess had the exact same form; for example, if the word is “running”, I advise that you consider “run” to be the correct guess.
