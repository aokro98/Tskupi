About the creator


Hi! My name is Anuka and I'm the creator of Tskupi. I am currently a Computer Science major, so as i gain more experience, I plan to keep working on Tskupi and turn what I envisioned into something you can enjoy.

Why Tskupi was made


Although Tskupi was my university project, I tried to incorporate details that were personal to me and I felt passionate about. For example, the recurring theme is something I put a lot of thought into.

When coming up with the project idea, my mind went to something I could personally appreciate. I thought of hanging out with friends and friends of friends, when you could really use an icebreaker, looking for ways to connect and have fun, going through classic party games, and searching online for something appropriate to play. That's when I thought of Tskupi, something you could be comfortable using to aid you in real-life fun, rather than online entertainment. To my mind, Tskupi is simply a way to help people bond.
